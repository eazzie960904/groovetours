About Groove Tours Company
Feel the Rhythm of Korea with GrooveTours Welcome to GrooveTours, where your journey through Korea becomes more than just sightseeing — it becomes a story worth remembering. We are a passionate team of locals and travelers who believe that the best way to experience a country is through its people, culture, and everyday life. Our mission is simple: to help global travelers discover the real Korea — beyond the guidebooks and tourist traps. From the buzzing energy of Seoul to the laid-back charm of Jeju Island, from the rich traditions of Jeonju to the dynamic coastal beauty of Busan, GrooveTours curates immersive experiences that connect you with the soul of each city.
What Makes Us Different?
Authentic Local Experiences
We work with local guides and communities to offer experiences you won’t find in mainstream packages. Whether it’s a hidden alley food tour, a sunrise hike with locals, or a private hanbok styling session in a historical village — we show you Korea through a different lens.

Cultural Connection, Not Just Travel
GrooveTours isn’t about checking off tourist spots — it’s about grooving with Korean culture. We focus on meaningful exchanges, small group tours, and hands-on experiences like Korean cooking classes, temple stays, and K-pop dance workshops.

Multi-City Adventures, Seamlessly Connected
Planning to explore more than one city? We’ve got you. Our tailored itineraries make it easy to hop from Seoul’s neon nights to Jeonju’s slow mornings, from Busan’s seaside energy to Jeju’s volcanic landscapes — all with comfort and style.

Friendly Guides, Global Perspective
Our bilingual (and sometimes trilingual!) guides are not just knowledgeable — they’re passionate storytellers who love sharing Korea with the world. Many have lived abroad or traveled extensively, so they understand both local context and your perspective.
Our Vision
We believe travel is a bridge — between cultures, people, and ideas. GrooveTours was founded to create those bridges, one memorable trip at a time.
Whether you're a solo traveler, a couple, or a group of friends, we want you to feel at home in Korea — and take a little bit of its groove back with you.
Let’s explore Korea together. Let’s groove.
"A Journey is More Than a Destination Some say travel is about seeing new places. We believe it’s about feeling something new. It’s the sound of your footsteps in a quiet Jeonju alley. The smell of street food wafting through a Seoul night market. The breeze on your face as you watch the sun rise over Jeju’s cliffs. The laughter shared with a stranger in a Busan cafe. At GrooveTours, we don’t just take you to Korea — we help you feel the rhythm of it. Because the best journeys don’t just stay in your photos. They stay in your heart."
